# Controlling Music

By default, most of our macropads are pre-configured to control music on your computer.

Setup buttons to play/pause music, skip to the next song and to go back to the previous song.

# Spotify

If you use Spotify, you can use these shortcuts to control the entire application. You can also use beatBind (opens new window) which allows global spotify shortcuts.
